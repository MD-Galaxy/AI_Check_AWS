# Email Provider Registration Guide

This guide walks through the registration process for two email service providers: **Alibaba Direct Mail** and **Submail**.

---

## 1. Alibaba Direct Mail

### Step 1: Open the Registration Page
Go to the Alibaba Cloud registration page:
https://account.alibabacloud.com/register/intl_register.htm?spm=a2c45.11132017.2.3.241e79f45PEj9x&oauth_callback=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.alibabacloud.com%2Fen%2Fproduct%2Fdirectmail%3F_p_lc%3D1

### Step 2: Create Your Account
1. Enter your **Email** and **Password**.
2. Click **Sign Up**.

### Step 3: Complete Security Verification
1. Select your **Country/Region**.
   - **Note:** India is not currently supported for registration.
2. Complete the verification using a phone number that matches the selected country/region (e.g., if you select Singapore, you must verify with a Singapore-registered phone number).

> **Important:** The country/region selected during registration is used for currency and tax purposes and **cannot be changed after registration**. Please choose carefully.

---

## 2. Submail

### Step 1: Open the Registration Page
Go to the Submail registration page:
https://global.mysubmail.com/registered

### Step 2: Create Your Account
1. Enter the following details:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email
   - Phone Number
2. Confirm the OTP sent to your phone/email.
3. Complete registration.

> **Note:** Registration is possible with an Indian phone number; however, a **business registration document** is required to use the service.

### Step 3: Complete Real-Name Authentication
After registration, you will be prompted to complete authentication:
1. Click **Go to Authentication** on the popup, or navigate to **Real-Name Authentication** in the left-hand panel.
2. Enter the required business details.
3. Submit for authentication approval.
