How it works
  1. Pick the email provider to send through — each provider uses its own credentials and sending domain.
  2. A unique email address is generated per conversation (encodes your name + a thread ID).
  3. The RFQ is sent from that address — supplier replies come back to the same address.
  4. The configured provider routes inbound replies to /webhooks/inbound, which parses and stores the reply.
  5. View the full conversation thread on the Tracking page.
